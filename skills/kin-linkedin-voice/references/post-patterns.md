## Post Templates (in Kin's voice)

### Revenue Intelligence — Text Post

```
[One statement about hotel revenue that most people get wrong — specific, not generic]

[2-3 sentences with a real example or data point. Name a company, cite a number.]

[The insight — what actually works vs. what people think works]

[Reframe or question that sends the reader into their own business]

#independenthotels #revenuemanagement #hotelrevenue
```

### Integration Gap — Text Post

```
[A scenario Kin has seen — specific enough to feel real, anonymized enough to be safe]

[What happened because revenue and marketing weren't connected]

[The fix — what changed when they got in the same room]

[One-line reframe]

#hotelmarketing #commercialstrategy #revenuemanagement
```

### Podcast Spotlight — Text Post (not Friday reshare)

```
[Guest name] told me something that stuck.

[The insight — paraphrased or quoted]

[Why it matters — Kin's reaction, what it changed in his thinking]

[Broader implication for hotel operators]

```

### Founder Journey — Text Post

```
[Personal observation or honest admission — something relatable]

[The story — what happened, what Kin learned]

[No wrapped-up lesson. Just the reflection, honestly stated.]

New episode with [Guest Name] on the Lights On Podcast. Link in comments.
```

## Carousel Posts (via Taplio)

- 6 slides optimal (engagement drops after slide 6-7)
- Slide 1: Bold hook statement
- Slides 2-5: One insight per slide, specific numbers
- Slide 6: Reframe + soft CTA
- No emoji bullet points
- Clean, scannable text per slide

## Comment Strategy Context

Kin comments 15-20 min daily on:
- Hotel owners/GMs who post
- HSMAI Hawaii chapter members
- Hospitality tech leaders (PMS CEOs, etc.)
- Revenue management community
- Podcast guests (past and future)

Comments should add value — specific insight, data point, or cross-industry analogy. Never "Great post!" and never pitch LOD.

## CTA Rules

- Max 1 soft CTA per week in posts
- Profile (About section, Featured) drives to discovery call — not the posts
- Soft CTA patterns:
  - "This is the kind of thing we catch in the first 20 minutes of a Revenue Growth Blueprint."
  - "Want us to run this analysis on your property? Link in my profile."
- Never in comments. Build authority; don't sell.
- Link in comments, not post body (external links reduce reach 40-50%)
